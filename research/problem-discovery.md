## Instructions

Each team member brainstorms 2-3 problems they've personally experienced. For each problem:

1. Describe the problem in 1 paragraph
2. Who has this problem?
3. What existing solutions exist?
4. What's the gap in existing solutions?
5. How would you solve it?
